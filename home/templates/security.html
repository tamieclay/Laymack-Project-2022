Who is LAYMACK...

Laymack (Pvt) Ltd Laymack Security Services was formed in year 2000 to provide a variety of security services in Zimbabwe. Since then, the company has penetrated the commercial, retail, domestic, manufacturing, mining and banking sectors. It is providing services which include guarding, roving investigations and escort of cash in transit. Its staff compliment is 60 and has the capacity to deploy many more guards into the market without compromising on quality service.

Our Vision is to be a prominent company offering all round security services & bringing positive impact through out the security Industry in Zimbabwe and beyond.

Laymack Security provides professional multi-disciplinary high quality services within the security sector.
